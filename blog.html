Indore to Maheshwar - Your Complete Travel Guide

Planning a trip from Indore to Maheshwar? Whether you prefer traveling by bus, train, cab, or car, here‚Äôs everything you need to know about the distance, routes, and travel options.

The distance between Indore and Maheshwar is approximately 95 km by road. If traveling by train, the nearest railway station is Barwaha, around 40 km from Maheshwar.

Indore to Maheshwar by Bus

There are multiple buses operating between Indore and Maheshwar. Popular services include AC and non-AC buses from AICTSL and private operators like Siddhivinayak and Chartered Bus.

Bus Timings: Buses are available throughout the day, with the earliest starting at 6 AM.
